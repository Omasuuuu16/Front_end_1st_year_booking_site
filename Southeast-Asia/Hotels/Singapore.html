Conrad Singapore Orchard

📍Orchard, Singapore

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Steps from Orchard Road shopping belt, Conrad Singapore Orchard features award-winning dining and a gym. Union Pay is accepted here. Conrad Singapore Orchard is 1.3 km from Singapore Botanic Gardens and 4.6 km from National Museum of Singapore. Singapore Flyer is a 10-minute drive away, while Universal Studios Singapore is 15 minutes by taxi. Rooms at Conrad Singapore Orchard feature Asian and Western designs. They include bathrooms with toiletries. Minibars and tea/coffee makers are provided. Rooms offer cable TV channels, including international channels. Bathrooms come with dental kit, slippers and toiletries. Guests can visit the tour desk to make sightseeing arrangements. The hotel offers laundry services. Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Other facilities include a 24-hour fitness center.

Marina Bay Sands

📍Marina Bay, Singapore

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Marina Bay Sands in Singapore offers a 5-star hotel experience with a rooftop swimming pool, fitness centre, restaurant, and bar. Guests enjoy sea views, a casino, and a variety of amenities including a hot tub and 24-hour front desk. The hotel features a buffet breakfast, brunch, lunch, and dinner with cuisines such as Chinese, British, French, and Italian. Dining venues include a steakhouse, local, and international options, catering to diverse preferences. Located in the city centre, Marina Bay Sands is a short walk from The Shoppes at Marina Bay Sands and Sands SkyPark Observation Deck. Changi Airport is 16 km away, and attractions like Gardens by the Bay are less than 1 km from the property.
