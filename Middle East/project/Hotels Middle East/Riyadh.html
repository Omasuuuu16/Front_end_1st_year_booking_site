The Ritz-Carlton

📍ِRiyadh

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Arched doorways and marble hallways define the interior of this luxurious Riyadh hotel, next to King Abdulaziz Convention Centre. The hotel features 6 restaurants, an indoor pool and spacious rooms with iPod docking stations. Free WiFi is available in public areas. The warmly decorated rooms at The Ritz-Carlton, Riyadh come with a satellite LCD TV and a CD/DVD player. The rooms also include a bathrobe with slippers, and a seating area to relax. An a la carte menu is offered at Azzurro resturant, The Ritz-Carlton's Italian restaurant. Guests can choose from a variety of International selections. The outdoor terrace offers a pleasant spot for afternoon tea or a light snack. Guests can also enjoy a wide selection of non-Cuban cigars.
