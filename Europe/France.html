Welcome to France's Highlights

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower, located in the heart of Paris, is an iconic symbol of France. Built in 1889 for the World's Fair, it stands as a marvel of engineering and offers breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks. more info

Mont Saint-Michel

Mont Saint-Michel is a stunning island commune in Normandy, famous for its medieval architecture and historic abbey. Surrounded by picturesque tides, it is a UNESCO World Heritage site that offers visitors a journey through history. more info

Palace of Versailles

The Palace of Versailles, once the residence of French kings, is a masterpiece of 17th-century architecture. Its lavish halls, sprawling gardens, and the iconic Hall of Mirrors showcase the grandeur of the French monarchy. more info
